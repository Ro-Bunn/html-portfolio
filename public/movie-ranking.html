The Best Books According to Rossan

My Top 3 Books of all-time

Throne of Glass by Sarah J Maas

This young adult fantasy series excels well in it's world building and makes you really care for it's each and individual character

The Fourth Wing by Rebecca Yarros

This Dragon Romantasy series has amazing world building, good amount of build-up and spice as well as an addictive storyline that keeps you hooked.

Yours Truly by Abigail Jimenez

This Contemporary Romance is everything you'll want in a romance. The main male character is perfect but in his own imperfect way. The characters don't feel surface level like all the other contemporary romances out there
